About Us

This extension was made during HackED Beta 2023. Which is an annual Hackathon hosted at the University of Alberta.

Why did we make this?

Don't you hate it when you have to read a long bloated article for an assignment? or just want to catch up with the times without wasting all of yours? We do too, so we created this extension to summarize articles and give you news based directly on your interests.
